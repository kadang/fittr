# fittr, a wellbeing action tracker

**So what's fittr?**

fittr is an app where you can track things you do for your fitness.

**Isn't it basically just a workout app?**

Well, yes and no.
You certainly *can* just track your workouts in fittr, but you can add other things, too.
I wanted something where I can add things I do for my wellbeing outside a normal workout program, such as going for a hike or taking the dog for a walk.
These activities contribute to your wellbeing as much as a good workout, if not more.
